# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to create an open and welcoming environment in our project and community. This means ensuring a harassment-free experience for everyone, respecting differences in age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Positive contributions to the community include demonstrating empathy and respect for others, being open to differing opinions, giving and accepting constructive feedback, and taking responsibility for mistakes. Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or advances, trolling, personal attacks, harassment, and sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for upholding standards of acceptable behavior and taking appropriate action against unacceptable behavior. They have the authority to remove or edit content that doesn't align with the Code of Conduct and will communicate reasons for these decisions.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and also when representing the project in public. This includes using official project communication channels or acting as a representative at events.

## Enforcement

Incidents of unacceptable behavior can be reported to community leaders responsible for enforcement at **[INSERT CONTACT METHOD]**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly, and community leaders are required to protect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will use the following guidelines to determine the appropriate response to violations:

### 1. Correction
For inappropriate or unwelcome language/behavior, a private written warning will be issued, explaining the violation and why it was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning
For a single incident or series of actions, a warning with consequences for further behavior will be issued. This may include a period of no interaction with involved parties or those enforcing the Code of Conduct.

### 3. Temporary Ban
For serious or sustained inappropriate behavior, a temporary ban from interacting with the community or representing the project will be implemented.

### 4. Permanent Ban
For a pattern of violations, harassment, or aggression, a permanent ban from the project community will be enforced.
